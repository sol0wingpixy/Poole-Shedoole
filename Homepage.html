Schedule Simulator

  • Course Selection

    1. As you open up the Schedule Simulator, select the Course Selection tab at the top left of the screen.
    2. Click the Classes tab to bring up the drop down menu of class subjects. You can pick up to 7 classes.
      1. When you click each subject, you can click the checkboxes that correspond to the classes you will take.
      2. If you are unsure about the descriptions for each class, hover your mouse over the individual classes and truncated course description of that class will be displayed.
    3. Then, click on the Sports and Clubs that you are hopeful in participating in.
    4. After you have completely selected all the classes, sports, and clubs you want, go to the top of the page and click the Generate tab.
    5. You will be directed to a two week example schedule of your freshman year.
      1. All activities are color coordinated and conflicts are shown in bright red.
